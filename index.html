About the Valued Living Questionnaires

The Valued Living Questionnaires (VLQ) are psychological assessment tools designed to help individuals explore and evaluate their personal values across different life domains. These questionnaires help you identify what is most important to you and assess how consistently you are living in accordance with your values.

VLQ-1: Basic Assessment

This questionnaire focuses on rating the importance of 10 life domains and how consistently your actions align with these values.

  • Rate the importance of each life domain
  • Assess consistency of your actions with your values
  • Visualize the relationship between importance and consistency
Start VLQ-1

VLQ-2: Comprehensive Assessment

This expanded questionnaire evaluates 12 life domains across six different aspects, providing a more detailed assessment of your valued living.

  • Evaluate possibility, importance, action, satisfaction, and concern
  • Prioritize areas for personal growth
  • Visualize complex relationships between different aspects
Start VLQ-2
